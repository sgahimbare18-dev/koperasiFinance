Our Loan Services

📄 Non-Member Plan

For Non-Members

  • Loan range: KES 10,000 – 200,000
  • Interest rate: 12% flat monthly
  • Collateral required (flexible assets)
  • Simple application process
✨ Special Consideration:
If you are a regular and known customer, higher loan amounts may be approved based on your relationship and history with us.
👥 Member Plan

For Members

  • Higher loan limits up to KES 500k+
  • Preferential terms (8% interest)
  • Faster approvals & lower fees
  • Reduced requirements + profit sharing
