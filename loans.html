Frequently asked questions

How fast can I get the money?▼
Members often receive funds within 24 hours after approval. Non-members typically 2-3 business days due to collateral verification.
Can I pay off my loan early?▼
Absolutely. No penalties, no extra fees. You only pay interest for the time you actually borrowed the money.
What documents do I need to apply?▼
Government ID, proof of income (or student status), and collateral documents for non-members. Members have reduced requirements.
How does profit sharing work for members?▼
Every 11 months, all interest collected from loans is distributed among members based on their contribution percentage.
